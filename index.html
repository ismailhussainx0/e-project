Dr. Abdus Salam

Pakistan's First Nobel Prize Winner

A visionary physicist whose work transformed modern particle physics and inspired generations.

Biography
The Journey of a Brilliant Physicist

Full Name: Dr. Mohammad Abdus Salam

Born: 29 January 1926 Jhang, Punjab, British India(now Pakistan)

Nationality: Pakistani

Field: Theoretical Physics

Nobel Prize: Winner of the Nobel Prize in Physics (1979)

Dr. Abdus Salam was a renowned Pakistani theoretical physicist and the first Pakistani to receive the Nobel Prize in Physics. He made groundbreaking contributions to particle physics and played a vital role in advancing scientific research worldwide. His dedication to education and science continues to inspire millions.
